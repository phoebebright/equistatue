About Skor.ie

There must be an easier way...

The first version was called DressageCalculator and was created in 2012 after Phoebe Bright, a developer and horse rider, was helping out at a dressage show doing the scoring of testsheets with a old fashioned calculator. After the fourth attempt at calculating the total of a sheet and getting a different answer, she vowed to create an app to take the pain out of calculating dressage scores!

Seven years later, DressageCalculator has grown into Skor.ie and has evolved into both a way of riders keeping a record of their scores and analysing their results and an event management application for dressage shows. The latest developments can be found at https://skor.ie while https://dressagecalculator.com remains the simple scoring webapp that does just what it says on the tin.
